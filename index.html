About Us

Geomad is at the forefront of agricultural innovation, providing cutting-edge remote sensing services to help farmers and agribusinesses optimize their operations. Our technology allows for precise monitoring and management of crops, leading to increased yields and reduced costs.

Our Services

  • Crop Health Monitoring: Use satellite imagery and drone technology to assess crop health in real-ime, enabling timely interventions.
  • Soil Moisture Analysis: Provide detailed soil moisture data to ensure optimal irrigation practices, conserving water and maximizing crop growth.
  • Weather Forecasting: Deliver precise weather forecasts tailored to your specific location to help you plan and protect your crops effectively.
  • Yield Prediction: Use advanced analytics to predict crop yields, allowing for better planning and market strategy.
  • Customized Agricultural Solutions: Offer bespoke solutions tailored to the unique needs of your farm, including precision farming techniques and data-driven insights.
